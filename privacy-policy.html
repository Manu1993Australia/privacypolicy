1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Marketing Insights Manu application ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and safeguards data accessed through the Meta (Facebook) Platform. The App is operated by Manuel Scheu for internal marketing analytics purposes across Jadore Hair Supplies, Hadrill Hair, and Emilly Hadrill Salons.

This is an internal business tool. It is not a consumer-facing product and is not designed to collect personal data from the general public.

2. What Data We Access

The App accesses the following data from the Meta Platform solely for business analytics and advertising performance purposes:

  • Advertising account performance metrics (impressions, clicks, spend, ROAS)
  • Campaign, ad set, and ad-level insights data
  • Page insights and audience data in aggregated, non-identifiable form
  • Business Manager account information associated with authorised ad accounts

We do not access, store, or process personal data of individual Facebook or Instagram users.

3. How We Use the Data

Data accessed through the App is used exclusively for:

  • Internal marketing performance reporting and analysis
  • Attribution analysis across advertising channels
  • Budget optimisation and campaign decision-making
  • Building internal dashboards for the above-named business entities

Data is never sold, licensed, or shared with third parties for commercial purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

Any data retrieved via the Meta API is processed in-memory or stored within secured, privately-operated infrastructure. We apply the following safeguards:

  • Access tokens are stored securely and never exposed in client-side code
  • Data is accessible only to authorised personnel within our business
  • We do not retain personally identifiable information from Meta's platforms
  • API access adheres to Meta's Platform Terms and Data Policy

5. Third-Party Services

This App integrates with the Meta Marketing API. Data handling by Meta is governed by Meta's Privacy Policy and Meta Platform Terms. We do not integrate with additional third-party data processors in relation to this App.

6. Data Retention

We retain aggregated analytics data for as long as necessary to support business reporting requirements, typically no longer than 24 months. Any access tokens or credentials are invalidated when no longer required or upon revocation.

7. User Data Deletion

As this App does not collect personal data from end users, there is no individual user data to delete. If you believe your data has been processed in connection with this App and wish to request deletion, please contact us at the email address below. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Your Rights

If you are an individual whose data may have been accessed in connection with this App, you have the right to:

  • Request information about what data (if any) we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of any personal data
  • Lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated accordingly. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
